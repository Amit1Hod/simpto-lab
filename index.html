1st Use Case: Endometriosis

Endometriosis is a chronic, inflammatory, estrogen-dependent gynecological condition affecting about 10% of women. It is a multi-system disease that manifests as pain in various organs both within and outside the pelvic region. As a result, each patient experiences a unique and wide range of symptoms.
